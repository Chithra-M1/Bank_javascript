Online Bank

An online bank is a financial institution that operates entirely through the internet, without traditional physical branches. Customers can manage their accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, apply for loans, and access other banking services using a website or mobile app. Online banks often offer lower fees and higher interest rates on savings accounts due to reduced overhead costs. They provide convenience and accessibility, allowing users to perform banking activities 24/7 from anywhere with an internet connection. However, they may rely on customer service through digital channels or phone rather than in-person interactions.
